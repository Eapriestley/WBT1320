Video: Here's What The Legend Of Zelda: Ocarina Of Time 3D Could Potentially Look Like On Switch

Just imagine 1080p, 60FPS

  • by Liam Doolan Tue 19th Jan 2021
  • Share:
  • 0

This year marks The Legend of Zelda's 35th anniversary. Much like Super Mario's celebrations, Nintendo could potentially revive any game in the series.

With this in mind, YouTuber SnazzyAI - the same individual who showed off Animal Crossing: New Horizons running at 4K, 60FPS - has released a new video, this time showing off what the 3DS title, The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time 3D could look like if it was somehow ported to the Nintendo Switch.

To do this, they took the "best trailer" of the 3DS game - resulting in 1080p, 60FPS.

"I took the best trailer I could find for it and actually downscaled it from 1080P (happens often where publishers will post it in higher quality than the video actually is) to 480P. Then AI upscaled it to 1080P from that 480P. Then AI interpolated it to 60FPS, as I think a higher frame rate is so needed if it were to come to the Switch."

While SnazzyAI would also welcome a full-on Switch remaster, they think most fans would settle for the 3DS version. And it would be rather fitting, considering the Switch is part portable.

Does this trailer make you want to return to this timeless classic? Would you like to see Ocarina of Time made available on the Switch at some stage? Let us know in the comments.
